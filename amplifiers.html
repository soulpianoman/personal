Background

Stereo has been a richly rewarding hobby for me over the years. The first time I heard an amazing system was in my fellow concert technician's NYC apartment. It was composed of a giant pair of Infinity IRS Beta speakers and a vinyl playback source powered by twin Audio Research tube amplifiers. This system rendered music such that the musicians appeared to be in the room giving the listener a personal concert! The intimacy and emotional impact it created left a lasting impression that planted the seeds for a journey down this audio rabbit-hole. A good friend helped me build my first tube amplifier which turned into many more. Above are some of the projects I have completed and been musically impressed with. Maybe sharing these photos will help others to generate some ideas and/or directions for their own DIY audio journey.
