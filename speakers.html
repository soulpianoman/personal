Background

Building speakers is just as much fun as building amps, although a little more physical! Above are some of the fun projects I have completed - many of which need a big room to reach their full potential. The magic combination for these ears have turned out to be very high-sensitivity speakers (preferrably great sounding horns) with single-ended class A amplifiers. (hopefully choke input power supplies). There are many roads to "audio bliss" and this has just poven to be one that appeals to me. Tubes and iron, tubes and iron. :)
